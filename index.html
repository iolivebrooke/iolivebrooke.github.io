About me

From product management to the classroom.

I spent years at Microsoft as a Product Manager, shipping software used by millions.

Today I teach AP Computer Science Principles and Visual Coding in JavaScript. I try to help my students learn to think like engineers, understand the societal impact of technology, and see themselves as people who belong in this field.

I also coach our school's powerlifting team!
